MediglyphCode

Medical IDs hold four conditions.
People live with more.

A standard medical bracelet gives a paramedic a name, a blood type, and a handful of words stamped into metal. No allergies. No contraindications. No context. When the person wearing it cannot speak, those gaps become clinical risk.

MediglyphCode encodes up to 145 medical conditions into a colour glyph the size of a thumbnail. Identity, triage priorities, chronic conditions, communication needs, and an emergency contact, all in 24mm × 14mm. Machine-readable by camera. Human-readable by design.
